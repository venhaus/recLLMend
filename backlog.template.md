# Backlog

> A holding area for items that aren't taste signal *yet* — owned-but-unrated, in progress, recommended-but-not-tried, queued, or abandoned without a verdict. **Not** the taste model: nothing here is scored, and no rating is ever invented. An item graduates to a real `log/` entry only when a genuine reaction (a rating + why, or a bounce) arrives. See `AGENTS.md`.
>
> This is the pristine template, owned by the upstream blueprint — never fill it with real data. On a fresh copy, create your live `backlog.md` by copying this file, then maintain that. (Sections are optional; drop any you don't use, add others if useful.)
